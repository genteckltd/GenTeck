Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers

Find quick answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and installation process.

We offer four main package categories: HomeConnect (25-100 Mbps for residential), BizPlus (100-500 Mbps for small business), Enterprise (500 Mbps-2 Gbps for large business), and EduConnect (specialized packages for educational institutions with content filtering and learning tools).

Fiber provides the fastest and most stable connection through underground cables, perfect for homes and businesses. 5G offers wireless connectivity with excellent mobility, ideal for areas where fiber installation isn't feasible. Both provide high-speed internet with 99.9% uptime guarantee.

No hidden fees! Our pricing is transparent. Installation is completely free for all packages. The only costs are your monthly subscription and any optional add-ons you choose (like static IP addresses or VPN services). We believe in honest, upfront pricing.

Yes! Pay annually and save 15% on your subscription. We also offer student discounts (10%), senior citizen discounts (10%), and business discounts for multiple locations (12%). Use our Bill Calculator to see your exact savings.

Most installations are completed within 24-48 hours of scheduling. Fiber installations typically take 2-4 hours, while 5G setup takes 1-2 hours. We'll conduct a free site survey first and schedule installation at your convenience.

Yes, someone needs to be present during installation for access and to test the connection. Our certified technicians will explain the setup, test all devices, and ensure you're completely satisfied before leaving. We also provide training on using your new connection.

We offer 24/7 customer support through live chat, phone, and email. Our technical team provides remote troubleshooting, on-site visits when needed, and priority support for business customers. Average response time is under 5 minutes.

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all our services. If we don't meet this standard, you'll receive account credits. Our redundant infrastructure and 24/7 monitoring ensure maximum reliability. Planned maintenance is always scheduled during low-traffic hours.
